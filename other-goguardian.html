fully remove your account from a chromebook (if you can't do that then powerwash)
log back into it
AS SOON AS YOU LOG BACK INTO IT, turn off your wifi
go to chrome://settings/signOut and click the button (before this, enter your sync passphrase if you have one)
turn wifi back on, goguardian won't work.

(optional) you will notice that your bookmarks and stuff haven't synced, here's how to fix this:
go to chrome://extensions and make sure goguardian is back
sign out of your chromebook (don't remove the account)
do steps 2-5 again

you will have to do steps 2-5 whenever you log out of your account or shut down your computer or else gg will come back in a few minutes and you'll have to do everything all over again

to revert back to normal, repeat steps 1 and 2
